Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to set up?
Most businesses are fully set up in under 10 minutes. Simply create your account, add your services and availability, and share your booking link. No technical knowledge required — if you can fill out a form, you can set up Appointly.
Can my clients book without creating an account?
Yes. Clients can book an appointment with just their name, email, and phone number. There's no account signup required on the client side, which dramatically reduces drop-off and makes the booking experience fast and frictionless.
How does payment collection work?
Appointly integrates directly with Stripe and PayPal. You can require full payment or a deposit at booking. Funds are deposited directly to your bank account. We charge zero transaction fees on top of Stripe's standard processing rates.
Can I manage multiple staff members and locations?
Absolutely. The Pro plan supports up to 5 staff members, and the Business plan supports unlimited staff across multiple locations. Each staff member has their own availability, services, and booking link. Clients can choose who to book with or let the system auto-assign.
What happens when I want to cancel?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings — no phone calls or hoops to jump through. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation, and we provide a full export of all your booking history and client data.
Is my data secure and GDPR compliant?
Security is a top priority. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. We are fully GDPR compliant, HIPAA-ready for healthcare providers, and host on SOC 2-certified infrastructure. We never sell or share your client data with third parties.
